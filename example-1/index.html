Example 1: Getting Started

In this first example, we'll look at a few major concepts in AngularJS:

Expressions
JavaScript-like code between two pairs of curly braces (e.g., {{ 2 + 2 }})
Filters
A way to transform your data. (e.g., myArray | filter:searchTerm)
Controllers
Manage your app's business logic
Data-binding
Keeps data in your controller in sync with your view (and vice-versa)
